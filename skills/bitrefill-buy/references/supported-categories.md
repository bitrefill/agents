## Category Notes

- **Country restrictions**: Many products are region-locked. Always verify the user's country matches the product.
- **Denomination ranges**: Gift card values typically range from $5 to $500, varying by brand.
- **Currency**: Prices are shown in the user's local currency on bitrefill.com. Crypto payments are converted at checkout.
